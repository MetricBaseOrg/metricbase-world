MetricBase World is a browser-based, isometric multiplayer MMO and living economic simulation. You play a chibi adventurer in a shared world: gather raw materials, craft them into gear, farm crops, fight monsters, trade with other players, and build a home of your own. Everything runs in your browser — no download required.

On top of the everyday loop sits a full open-world PvP layer: danger zones with real loot stakes, deep gear with armour, crit and weapon abilities, guild warfare (ranks, banks, tax, wars), territory control, and a weekly-style Castle Siege for the crown. Fight only where you choose — towns stay safe.

And when you're ready to leave your mark: found a Player World 🌍 — your own buildable zone with farms, markets, rivers and bridges — charge visitor passes and gather tax, and grow it by burning $BASE. This page is the full wiki: everything in the game is documented below.

🚀 1. Getting Started

MetricBase World is gated by the $BASE token on Solana to keep the economy healthy.

1
Open the game Go to world.metricbase.org in a modern desktop or mobile browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari).
2
Connect your Solana wallet Click Connect Wallet and approve the signature request. This proves you own the wallet — it does not move any funds.
3
Hold at least 1,000 $BASE Your wallet needs a minimum balance of the $BASE token to enter the world. This is the world's "key" — your tokens stay yours.
4
Name your character & spawn in Pick a name and you'll appear in the MetricBase Hub with 25 starting gold. Your progress (level, gold, inventory, skills, quests, land) is saved automatically.
Your character is tied to your wallet, so you can log back in from any device and pick up exactly where you left off.

🔄 3. The Core Loop

MetricBase World is built around one satisfying everyday loop, wrapped in a community layer:

⛏️ Gather

Chop wood, mine ore, catch fish, and harvest crops to collect raw materials.

🔨 Craft

Turn materials into planks, bars, food, tools, and weapons at the workbench.

💰 Trade

Sell to Pip, run your own shop, or swap gold for $BASE on the open market.

🏠 Build

Buy land, build a house or shop, and make the world your own.

🤝 Community

Form guilds and parties, chat, emote, and climb the leaderboards together.

⛏️ 4. Gathering

Walk up to a resource node, face it, and press the gather key. There are three gathering skills, each with its own XP and levels:

SkillNodeKeyYields
WoodcuttingTreesFWood, Hardwood
MiningRocks & depositsFCopper Ore, Iron Ore
FishingFishing spots (on water)G12 fish species across 5 rarities

Tiers & requirements

Higher-tier nodes need a higher skill level — e.g. Iron Deposits and Hardwood trees require level 3, and Ironwood requires level 4. Level a skill up by gathering its basic nodes first.

Rare finds ✨

Every gather has a small chance to also turn up a rare material — Amber (trees), Gemstone (rocks), or Pearl (fish). They sell for a lot, and a Gemstone can be forged into the best weapon in the game.

Equip a crafted tool (axe, pickaxe, fishing rod, or net) to gather faster — and steel-tier tools also have a chance to drop a bonus resource each time. See Crafting & Tools.

🎣 Fishing is a minigame

Unlike trees and rocks, fish fight back. Cast at a fishing spot and:

  • Wait for the bite — tap too early and you spook it (the wait restarts).
  • ❗ BITE! — hook it within the reaction window.
  • Reel — tap when the sweeping marker is inside the green zone. Three good taps land the fish; two misses and the line snaps.

Better rods widen the green zone, and rain makes it wider still — the fish really do bite better in the rain. Every cast costs 2g of bait and the energy is spent landed or escaped — so breathe, watch the rhythm, and tap on the beat.

🐠 Fish rarity

Every landed fish rolls which species it is. Most catches are the common River Fish or Prized Salmon, but rarer species surface too — and they sell to Pip for far more. A Lucky Lure and rain both tilt the odds toward rarer species, and rare-and-better catches are announced to the whole zone.

🔨 6. Crafting & Tools

Open the Crafting panel (C or the 🔨 button) to turn raw materials into useful goods. Crafting charges a small forge fee in gold and takes time at the workbench — heavier gear takes longer to forge, and the button shows "Crafting…" while it works.

What you can make

  • Materials: Planks, Hardwood Planks, Copper / Iron / Steel Bars, and Lamp Oil (fuel for building lights).
  • Food: Cooked Fish, Bread, Grilled Salmon — restore both HP and energy.
  • Weapons: Copper Dagger (+18), and the prestige Gemforged Blade (+30, needs a rare Gemstone).
  • Tools: three tiers that speed up gathering.

Fishing accessories

Craftable gear that boosts fishing while worn (stacks with your rod):

  • 📿 Lucky Lure Pendant (necklace) — rare catches (pearls) surface much more often. Craft: 2 Pearls + 1 Iron Bar.

The Gilded Rod is crafted from planks, steel and rare Amber; the Abyssal Rod upgrades a Gilded Rod with 3 Pearls — the ultimate fishing setup also widens the catch minigame's green zone.

⚔️ 7. Combat & Leveling

Approach a hostile creature (Training Dummy, Wild Slime, Slime Brute) and press Space to attack. The server handles all damage fairly. Monsters hit back, so watch your HP.

⚠️ Monsters hit hard. Every creature counter-attacks when you trade blows, and they hit for serious damage — the Slime Brute in the Grotto can take down an under-levelled adventurer fast. Don't out-punch what you can't out-heal: keep food on hand, level up, and equip the best weapon you can craft before taking on the Brute.
  • Equip a weapon for more damage per hit.
  • Earn XP and gold from defeating monsters (and quests), which raises your combat Level and max HP — all the way to level 50. Your gathering skills climb to 50 too.
  • Out of combat, your HP slowly regenerates — or eat food to heal fast.

Knockout & respawn

If your HP hits 0 you're knocked out. Pay 100 gold to respawn immediately, or wait 30 minutes for a free revival. You can't move or fight while knocked out.

⚔️ Gear, armour & abilities

Damage is Attack × Skill × Crit − Armour. Armour has diminishing returns — it always helps but never makes you invincible. Base crit is 5% at 1.5×; gear raises both.

  • Equipment slots: weapon, tool, helmet, chest, gloves, boots, two rings, necklace, cape, offhand. Craft copper / iron / steel armour sets plus a gemstone ring, pearl amulet, and cape.
  • Rarity: gear comes in 8 tiers (Common → Unique, shown by name colour) that scale its stats.
  • Durability: weapons and armour wear as you fight and break at 0. Use 🔧 Repair all gear in the Inventory panel for a gold fee.
  • Weapon abilities: each weapon type fills the skill hotbar (1–5 / Q / E / R) with its own kit — swap weapons to swap abilities.

The Inventory panel shows your live ⚔️ attack · 🛡️ armour · 🎯 crit · 💥 crit power and a durability bar for each piece.

🗡️ 7b. PvP & Danger Zones

Player-vs-player combat is opt-in by where you go. Every zone shows a danger tier banner when you enter:

TierWhereRules
🟢 SafeHub, Community LodgeNo PvP, ever.
🟡 YellowWildernessOpt-in: toggle your PvP flag; both fighters must be flagged. No item loss.
🔴 RedSlime GrottoOpen PvP. On death you drop your gathered materials; gear survives.
⚫ BlackObsidian ReachFull loot — drop your whole bag + half your loose gold. Richest rewards.

To fight: left-click a player to target them, then attack. When someone is knocked out in a Red/Black zone, their dropped items form a loot bag anyone can grab with F.

🛡️ Anti-grief built in: players under level 5 can't be pulled into PvP, you get a few seconds of spawn immunity after entering a zone, and Safe zones are always off-limits to fighting.

👮 Crime, reputation & bounties

Strike down a non-consenting player and you become a Criminal — your name turns red and towns lock you out until it cools off (~10 min). Anyone can put a bounty (gold) on a target; whoever defeats them claims the pot. Guild-war kills are sanctioned and don't make you a criminal.

⛓️ Jail

Crime doesn't pay: a criminal who's defeated in PvP gets arrested and thrown in the Jail for 2 minutes. The cell stays locked until your sentence is served — then you're released and pardoned, free to return to town.

🤺 Duels (Arena)

Want a fair fight with nothing on the line? Left-click a player and hit ⚔️ Duel. If they accept, you both heal to full and fight a consensual 1v1 anywhere — even in a safe town — with no loot, no crime, and no knockout penalty. Drop your opponent to win; the loser is simply restored. Duels draw after 90 seconds, and leaving forfeits.

🎯 Reading a fight

PvP is fully readable: hurt or PvP-flagged players show an HP bar over their head, and while you're duelling or trading blows a target frame appears at the top of the screen with your opponent's HP, energy, and level. Eating or drinking is public — everyone nearby sees the item bubble, and the target frame lists what your opponent just used. Time your burst for when the potion chugger runs dry.

🐎 Mounts

Craft a mount — Sturdy Pony (+25%), Swift Steed (+45%), or Dire Wolf (+70%) — and equip it in the Inventory panel's Mount slot for a permanent movement-speed boost.

🛡️ 7c. Guild Warfare & Territory

Guilds are now full power structures. Open the 🛡️ Guild panel to manage yours.

  • Approval-gated joins: request to join a guild and a leader or officer must accept you — no instant joins. Your request shows as "Pending" until then.
  • Ranks: Leader 👑 / Officer ⭐ / Member. The leader promotes, demotes, and kicks; officers approve/deny join requests, kick members, and run the bank & wars.
  • Guild bank: any member deposits gold; officers and the leader withdraw.
  • Income tax: the leader sets 0–10%; a slice of every member's gold earnings auto-funds the bank.
  • Wars: declare war on a rival guild (mutual). Warring guilds fight each other freely — even in Yellow zones without flagging — and those kills are lawful. Make peace to end it.

🏴 Territory control

PvP zones contain capture points. Stand on one as a guild member for about 12 seconds uncontested to claim it — a rival nearby contests it and freezes progress. A flag shows the owning tag, a capture ring, and contested state. Every point your guild holds pays territory income into the guild bank every 5 minutes. Ownership persists; disband and you lose your land.

👑 7d. Castle Siege

On a recurring schedule (a 15-minute window every 4 hours), the King Crystal in the Obsidian Reach (Black Zone) becomes vulnerable and a red “CASTLE SIEGE LIVE” banner appears.

  • Guild members click the crystal to strike it during the window.
  • The crystal is heavily armored with a huge HP pool — one player cannot break it inside the window. Bring guildmates, your best weapons, and plenty of food (every strike costs energy).
  • The guild that lands the killing blow is crowned Sovereign of MetricBase until the next siege, wins a large prize into its guild bank, and is announced server-wide.
  • The reigning Sovereign shows in a 👑 chip and persists across restarts; the crystal resets each window.
Reaching the crystal means braving the full-loot Black Zone — bring your guild, your best gear, and watch for rivals.

🥇 PvP seasons & rank

Every PvP knockout shifts your PvP rating (+25 for a kill, −15 for a death), which sets your rank — Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Diamond, Master, Grandmaster, Legend. The 🏆 Leaderboard's PvP tab ranks the season's best fighters. Seasons last 90 days; at each reset your rating returns to the baseline (you keep your character, gear, and gold).

💰 9. Trade & Economy

Pip's Provisions (Hub)

Press E near Pip to buy supplies (potions, seeds, a starter blade) and sell your gathered & crafted materials for gold.

Pip's buy prices are dynamic: the more of a material is sold, the less he pays for it (prices recover over time). It keeps the economy from flooding — sell a variety, not just one thing.

The open Gold Market

Pip's shop also hosts a player-to-player order book where you trade in-game gold for crypto. Pick your currency — $BASE, USDC, IDRX, or SOL — then post a bid (buy gold) or an offer (sell gold). Trades settle peer-to-peer on-chain directly between the two wallets, verified by the server; a small in-game gold fee applies. Each order shows the currency it's priced in, so check before you buy.

📈 Price chart & recent trades

The market tab shows a live candlestick price chart (tokens per 1 gold) plus the last price and 24h change, so you can spot the going rate before you trade. The chart follows the currency you've selected — switch the currency picker to see each market's own history. Underneath, a Recent transactions ticker lists the latest fills across every currency, each with the gold traded, the amount and currency it settled in, and how long ago it happened.

📣 9b. Ads & Earnings

MetricBase World has a built-in ad marketplace: brands buy ad space in the world, and players share 50% of the revenue just by playing. Open it from ⚙️ → 📣 Ads & Earnings. All money is in $BASE.

💸 Earn as a player

Ads appear on in-world billboards (one in each zone) and a banner above your skill bar. Every minute you can see an ad, you generate an impression — and as a program member you earn 50% of what the advertiser pays for it.

1
Invite 5 friends Your invite count shows in the Earn tab (X / 5).
2
Apply to the program Once you've invited 5 friends, tap Apply to join.
3
Play & earn Earnings accrue automatically from ads you see. Track your claimable balance, lifetime earnings, and impressions.
4
Claim in $BASE Cash out to your wallet once you reach the 10,000 $BASE minimum.

📈 Advertise as a brand

🏢 No game account needed! Brands have their own portal at world.metricbase.org/brands — connect a wallet, deposit $BASE, and launch a campaign with a live billboard preview in about three minutes. Everything below also works from the in-game 📣 Ads panel.

Deposit $BASE, create a campaign, and bid:

  • Deposit $BASE (min 100) to fund your ad balance. Paid already but don't see it? Use "Credit a past deposit" with your transaction hash.
  • Create a campaign — name, image URL, headline, click URL, and a CPM bid ($BASE per 1,000 impressions). It goes to review.
  • Win the best slots — once approved, ads are ranked by CPM. The highest bid takes the highest-traffic slot (the Hub billboard), then the banner, then the other zones. Higher bid = more visibility.
Every zone's billboard and the banner always show an ad — when a slot has no ad of its own it displays the top-ranked campaign, so a single advertiser can reach the whole world.

📅 9c. Daily Rewards

Open ⚙️ → 📅 Daily Rewards for free gold every day:

  • 🔥 Login streak — claim a bonus each day you log in. It grows with consecutive days: 100g → 150g → 200g → 300g → 400g → 500g → 1,000g from day 7 on. Miss a day and the streak resets.
  • Three daily tasks — rotate at midnight UTC from a pool: gather 🧺, defeat mobs ⚔️, craft 🔨, harvest crops 🌾, sell items 💰, or visit a Player World 🌍 (that one pays a 💎 gem too). Progress counts automatically while you play — just come back and press Claim.
The "visit a player World" task is the easiest gold of the day — open 🌍 Worlds → Explore and hop into any published World.

🧑‍🌾 9d. Job Board — hire & be hired

Open ⚙️ → 🧑‍🌾 Job Board to hire other players or work for them. It works in every zone and inside player Worlds.

  • 📦 Deliver items — the employer requests a quantity of any stackable material or consumable (wood, ore, fish, potions…). The worker gets the items into their bag and presses Deliver; partial deliveries are fine. The employer collects the delivered items from the panel.
  • 🧺 Gather / 🌾 Harvest / ⚔️ Defeat mobs — activity contracts. Progress counts automatically while the worker plays, anywhere in the world, exactly like daily tasks.

How the money moves: the gold reward is taken from the employer and held in escrow the moment the job is posted — a worker can never be stiffed. When the job completes, the reward is paid to the worker automatically (by mail-safe credit if they're offline) and the employer gets a 📬 mail notification. Cancelling an open job refunds the escrow in full.

  • Each player can have up to 5 active job postings and work 1 job at a time.
  • Rewards range from 10g to 1,000,000g. Abandoning an activity job resets its progress for the next worker; delivered items always stay with the employer.
  • All job activity is tracked publicly on the 📊 Economy dashboard.
New player with 25g? Take a delivery job — buy or gather the items, deliver, and pocket the reward. It's the fastest way to earn from other players' gold.

🏠 10. Housing & Shops

Owning land is the big mid-game goal (and the largest gold sink). Plots are available in every outdoor zone.

1
Buy a plot Stand on an empty 3×3 land plot, interact, and buy it for 500 gold.
2
Build a House or a Shop Your name goes on it, and it becomes a solid structure others can see.
3
Make it yours Repaint the roof (6 colours), put a custom name on the sign, and place props on the four corners.

😴 Rest at home

Your house isn't just for show — it's where you recover. Open your own house (🏠 panel) and hit Rest here to instantly refill your energy and HP. You can rest again every 8 minutes, so a home is a real advantage for long play sessions (you'll still want food on the go).

💡 Building lights

Switch your house or shop's light on from the 🏠 panel. A lit building casts a warm window glow that everyone can see at night — great for finding your place (or your shop) after dark. The light slowly burns the building's energy reserve while it's on; top it up with Lamp Oil, a craftable fuel (2 River Fish + 1 Wood at the workbench — each flask restores 50 energy). When the reserve runs out, the light switches off until you refuel.

Run a player shop

Build a Shop and stock items from your inventory at prices you choose. Anyone can buy from it (by quantity or buy-all). Sales pile up as earnings you collect on your next visit — so you earn even while offline.

🤝 11. Social Play

🛡️ Guilds

Found a guild for 1,000 gold, or request to join one — a leader or officer must approve your request. Your tag shows on your nameplate worldwide, with a shared roster, bank, ranks, wars, and cross-zone guild chat.

🎉 Parties

Invite up to 3 others into a party. Fight or gather near your partymates for bonus XP — the finisher of a kill earns extra, allies share assist XP and quest credit, and nearby members share gathering XP.

💬 Chat

Toggle between Zone, Guild, and Party channels. Guild and party messages reach members across zones. Tag anyone with @Name (or tap a name in chat) — mentions highlight for them and ring their 🔔 bell, which also collects new mail and duel challenges.

😀 Emotes & Who

Broadcast emoji bubbles, and open the online roster to see who's around and their levels.

🏆 Leaderboard

Climb the top-10 charts for combat Level, Richest (gold), gathering Skills, and seasonal PvP rating.

📢 Hub Billboard

The plaza billboard shows the live on-chain $BASE holder count and how many players are online.

✨ 11b. VIP Lodge, Arcade & the Black Zone

🏛️ Community Lodge (VIP)

The Community Lodge off the Hub is VIP-only, with three ways in (prompted at the door, saved to your character): hold 10,000,000 $BASE (free while you hold), a Burn pass — 100 gold + burn 10,000 $BASE for 14 days, or a Gold pass — 1,000 gold (no burn) for 14 days. Inside, talk to the arcade host to launch the Base Rush arcade game in a full-screen cabinet.

⚫ The Black Zone (Obsidian Reach)

Reached through the Black Gate in the Slime Grotto. Entry costs a one-time burn of 1,000,000 $BASE — a lifetime unlock tied to your account. It's full-loot and holds the best resources in the game (obsidian gemstone veins, ancient hardwood, dense iron) plus the King Crystal and its weekly siege.

$BASE burns and VIP holdings are real on-chain actions, verified by the server. Burning is permanent — make sure you mean it before you confirm.

🌍 12. Player Worlds — build your own zone

The end-game flex: found your own World — a persistent, buildable zone that other players can visit, farm, and shop in. You design it, you set the prices, you collect the earnings.

1
Found a World Open 🌍 Worlds → My Worlds → Found a World. A slot costs 1,000,000 gold (paid to the game treasury). Short on gold? Pip's gold desk in the same panel sells gold 1:1 for $BASE.
2
Build it Enter your World and hit 🔨 Build. Drag assets from the palette onto the map (or tap-select then tap tiles). Tools: ✋ Move (drag placed objects to a new tile), 🧹 Erase, 📍 Spawn (where visitors arrive), and 💾 Save. A walkability guide tints blocked tiles red and rivers blue while you build.
3
Publish & earn Name it, set a visitor pass price (up to 100,000g, 0 = free) and a gather tax (up to 25% of the value visitors earn per harvest, at shop prices), then Publish. Pass sales and taxes pile into your earnings — hit Collect anytime.
⚔️ Set your World's PvP danger tier — 🟢 Safe (no PvP), 🟡 Yellow (opt-in, both must flag), 🔴 Red (open PvP, drop gathered loot on death), or 🟣 Black (full loot — drop everything). Pick it in the Worlds manage card; visitors see the tier in the directory and are warned the instant you change it. Higher risk makes a great arena or a dangerous farming ground — your call.

🧱 Assets, the Build Shop & the P2P market

  • Grass and bare dirt are free; other ground is cheap; decor is moderate; buildings cost thousands; resource nodes are the priciest (they earn forever).
  • Buy assets for gold in the 🛒 Build Shop — they go to your asset inventory, are consumed when placed, and return to inventory when removed.
  • Trade assets with other players on the built-in P2P market (list for gold; escrowed until sold or cancelled).
  • Saving a build auto-buys anything you placed but don't own, and refunds removals.

🌊 Terrain that plays

  • Rivers block walking (they're 2×2 tiles) — lay a Bridge over one to open a crossing. Design chokepoints and routes!
  • Soil is a working farm plot: visitors plant seeds and harvest, and each harvest pays you the gather tax.
  • Buildings block their footprint (homes 3×3, markets/windmill/bridge 2×2); trees, rocks, and decor stay walkable. Resource nodes you place are really gatherable — wood, ore, and fishing spots all work.
  • Mob dens spawn real monsters — place a 🟢 Slime Den (Wild Slime) or a Brute Den (Slime Brute) and visitors can fight them for combat XP and loot (slime gel / cores — no direct gold, so Worlds can't be farmed as a gold faucet). They respawn like any mob and count toward the ⚔️ daily task.
  • The Shop building is a working store — walk up + interact to browse Pip's full general store (same stock, prices, and price pressure as the Hub), so visitors can restock potions and sell their haul without leaving your World.

🌾 Crop Markets

Place a Wheat Market or Carrot Market and it becomes a working trade post: visitors walk up + interact to buy that crop's seeds and sell their harvest (wheat: 5g seed / 7g sell · carrot: 9g / 14g). Pair markets with soil fields for a self-contained farming economy that ticks daily quests too.

🌗 Day & night

The world runs on a live day/night cycle — a full day passes about every 20 minutes. Watch the sky shift through dawn ☀️ day, dusk 🌇, and night 🌙; the HUD clock shows the current in-world time. Everyone shares the same time of day.

🔦 Light up the night. Press L (or tap the 💡 button) to toggle your lamp. It casts a warm glow around you that cuts through the dark — and other players can see your light too. Gathering is slower at night unless your lamp is on, so it's more than just decoration. Turn it off in daylight when you don't need it.

🌦️ Weather

The skies are alive too. The world rolls through weather — clear 🌤️, cloudy ☁️, rain 🌧️, fog 🌫️, and the occasional thunderstorm ⛈️ with lightning. Like the time of day, everyone shares the same weather, and the HUD shows the current conditions. (Rain stays outside — step into the Community Lodge to dry off.) Weather isn't just for show: fish bite more in the rain, so grab your rod when the clouds roll in. You'll also hear it — a soft rain bed and rolling thunder in storms (rides the 🎵 music toggle).

💡 15. Tips for New Players

  • Do Aria's starter quests first — they teach the basics and hand you early gold.
  • Gather a mix of wood, ore, and fish, then craft a tool early — faster gathering snowballs everything else.
  • Plant a crop before you log off; it'll be ready when you return.
  • Don't dump 20 of one item on Pip at once — his prices drop. Spread your sales.
  • Save toward a 500-gold land plot; a player shop earns passive income.
  • Team up — a party levels faster thanks to shared combat and gather XP.
  • Keep some food on hand so a tough fight doesn't end in a knockout.
  • Always carry food — it refills your 🍗 energy so you can keep gathering, fighting, and farming. Bread is the cheap staple; cooked fish and salmon give more.
  • Claim your 📅 Daily Rewards every day — the login streak alone is up to 1,000g/day, and the "visit a World" task is free money.
  • Visit Player Worlds with low gather tax for fresh nodes — and when you're rich, found your own and make the tax work for you.
