The pipeline

four moves, on repeat
01

Collect signals

Scouts watch X and blockchain data around the clock for the earliest sign of a new Solana game — a launch post, a fresh token, a wallet waking up. The faintest signal is enough to open a file.

02

AI agents enrich

Each candidate gets its own AI agent. It reads the game, checks there’s a real playable client, confirms it actually lives on Solana, pins down the true launch date, resolves the token, and profiles the team.

03

Verify & rank

The agent’s findings are cross-checked against live blockchain market data — price, liquidity, holders — and scored by social buzz, then ranked so the real movers rise to the top.

04

Keep watching

Agents never stop monitoring. Big moments become events on the live feed; games that go quiet are marked dormant — and the moment one relaunches, it’s back.

What the labels mean

the tags you’ll see on Discover
Scouted

Just spotted from a fresh signal on X or on-chain — queued for an AI agent, not yet investigated.

Analyzed

AI agents read the game, confirmed it’s a real playable Solana game, anchored its launch, resolved its token and profiled the team.

Verified

Analyzed and double-confirmed as authentic — the highest-trust tier.

Rejected

Screened out — not a game, not on Solana, vaporware, or no fresh launch.

Dormant

Was a real game, now gone quiet — deprioritized, but agents keep watching for a relaunch.

What “Buzz” means

the metric the whole site runs on

Buzz is your instant read on the games the world is actually paying attention to. We listen across social in real time and distill all that chatter into one live score. Games gaining genuine momentum rise to the top; the quiet ones drift down. Higher Buzz simply means more real attention, right now — so you can spot what’s heating up at a glance, before the crowd piles in.

Real voices count most

Buzz weighs who’s actually talking, not just how many. Attention from credible voices the gaming world listens to carries real weight.

Genuine excitement, not noise

It rewards real reactions from real people — the posts that spark conversation — rather than empty numbers anyone can pad.

Tuned to right now

Buzz is built around live momentum, so a game that’s taking off surfaces fast — and one that’s gone quiet quietly drifts back down.

Built to resist hype

Bought followers and coordinated shilling are filtered out, so the top of the board reflects genuine interest — not whoever paid the most.
